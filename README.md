# 🗺️ Vendor Map

A real-time local discovery platform helping customers find small businesses, pop-up vendors, and independent sellers.

🔗 **Live Website:**
https://vendor-map-mu.vercel.app/

Vendor Map is an early-stage MVP prototype designed to explore how location-based discovery can help small businesses improve visibility while helping customers discover local vendors more easily.

The idea behind Vendor Map is simple:

Small businesses often have great products and services, but customers do not always know:

* Who is selling nearby
* Which vendors are currently available
* Where businesses are located
* Which businesses they want to revisit

Vendor Map creates a connection between local businesses and customers through real-time discovery.

# 🌱 Why I Built Vendor Map

Many small businesses and independent vendors rely on:

* Social media algorithms
* Word of mouth
* Flyers
* Community events
* Foot traffic

However, these methods do not always make discovery easy.

Customers may want to support local businesses but have no simple way to know:

* Who is nearby
* Which vendors are currently active
* Where to find them
* Which businesses they want to support again

Vendor Map was created to explore a simple question:

> What if discovering local businesses was as easy as opening a map?

This MVP focuses on validating the core experience before expanding into a larger marketplace platform.

---

# 🎯 MVP Goal

The first version focuses on testing whether real-time vendor discovery creates value for both customers and businesses.

The core experience:

1. Customers discover vendors
2. Customers view business information
3. Customers save businesses they like
4. Vendors share when they are actively selling
5. Customers discover local businesses in real time

The goal is to learn from real users and build based on actual needs.

# ✨ Features

## 🗺️ Interactive Vendor Discovery Map

Users can browse local businesses through an interactive map experience.

Features:

* Google Maps integration
* Vendor location markers
* Business information previews
* Real-time vendor visibility
* Location-based discovery

---

## 📱 Phone Authentication

Users can create accounts using phone authentication with SMS verification.

Benefits:

* Faster onboarding
* No passwords required
* Easier experience for vendors
* Designed for simple onboarding

Future authentication options:

* Google login
* Email login

---

## 🏪 Vendor Business Profiles

Authenticated users can create and manage business profiles.

Current business information:

* Business name
* Category
* Description
* Owner account
* Location
* Live status

Future improvements:

* Business photos
* Social media links
* Product listings
* Inventory management
* Business hours
* Contact information

---

## 🟢 Go Live Feature

Vendors can temporarily appear on the map when they are actively selling.

Workflow:

1. Vendor creates a business profile
2. Vendor selects "Go Live"
3. Location permission is requested
4. Current location is saved
5. Vendor appears on the map

When vendors are not live:

* Their business remains discoverable
* Customers can save them
* Their profile remains available

---

## ❤️ Saved Vendors

Customers can save businesses they want to revisit.

Saved vendors remain available even when offline.

This creates a relationship between customers and businesses beyond a single visit.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Vendor Dashboard

Current functionality:

* Update descriptions

* Delete businesses

Future improvements:

* Upload photos

* View analytics
* Customer engagement tracking
